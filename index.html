Homepage of Mark Ioppolo


I am a mathematician and programmer from Perth, Western Australia. My interests are information theory, coding theory, cryptography and and their connections with geometry and abstract algebra.

I completed my PhD at the UWA Centre for Mathematics of Symmetry and Computation under John Bamberg, Alice Devillers and Cheryl E. Praeger. My thesis is titled "Codes in Johnson graphs associated with quadratic forms over GF(2)". A technical summary is available here.
